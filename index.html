Critically Endangered Animals at the STL Zoo

This project was created to raise awareness about the urgent need to protect the world's most vulnerable and endangered animal species.

‎

At the Saint Louis Zoo, they are committed to protecting animals and their habitats, both locally and globally. They believe that it is their responsibility to work towards preventing animal extinction and building strong, sustainable communities that coexist with wildlife.

‎

Sadly, many species are facing extinction due to a variety of factors, such as habitat destruction, climate change, poaching, and pollution. We must act now to save these animals and preserve our planet's biodiversity for future generations.
